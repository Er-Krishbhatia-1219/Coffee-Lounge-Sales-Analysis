# ☕ Coffee Lounge Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard for a coffee lounge business using Microsoft Excel.

The dashboard provides business insights related to:
- Sales performance
- Customer footfall
- Product demand
- Operational trends

The aim of this project is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence that helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

# 📌 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ Quantity Orders Based on Hours

- Identifies peak customer ordering hours
- Highest order activity observed during morning hours

---

## 2️⃣ Product Sales Based on Category

### Categories Analyzed:
- Coffee
- Bakery
- Tea
- Drinking Chocolate
- Coffee Beans
- Loose Tea
- Flavours

Coffee contributes the highest percentage of total sales.

---

## 3️⃣ Footfall & Sales on Franchise Outlets

### Franchise Locations:
- Astoria
- Hell's Kitchen
- Lower Manhattan

Hell's Kitchen generated the highest revenue.

---

## 4️⃣ Top 5 Best Sellers

Top-performing products:
- Barista Espresso
- Brewed Chai Tea
- Gourmet Brewed Coffee
- Hot Chocolate

---

## 5️⃣ Weekday Sales Analysis

- Friday recorded the highest sales
- Weekend sales were comparatively lower

---

## 6️⃣ Percentage Size Distribution

Order sizes analyzed:
- Large
- Regular
- Small
- Undefined

Regular-sized orders had the highest contribution.

# 🔍 Business Insights

- Coffee category contributes approximately 39% of total sales
- Morning hours are the busiest operational period
- Hell's Kitchen outlet performs best in terms of revenue
- Espresso-based beverages are customer favorites
- Sales are strongest on weekdays, especially Fridays
